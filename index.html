Professional Grade Formula

Elements Topical Fluoride Thixotropic Gel (Strawberry) is a high-potency 1.23% acidulated phosphate fluoride (APF) gel designed to prevent dental caries in children and adults.

With its thixotropic nature, the gel flows easily into interproximal areas and stays in place without dripping, ensuring optimal fluoride contact. The delicious strawberry flavor enhances patient compliance.

Key Features

  • 1.23% Acidulated Phosphate Fluoride
  • Thixotropic gel with strawberry flavor
  • Non-drip, smooth application
  • Suitable for all age groups
  • pH ~3.5 for enhanced uptake
  • Multidose dispensing bottle

How to Use

  1. Dry teeth thoroughly
  2. Apply gel in trays or directly with brush
  3. Leave in contact for 1-4 minutes
  4. Instruct patient not to rinse or eat for 30 minutes

Applications

Fluoride therapy in pediatric and adult patients • Use after scaling or prophylaxis • Caries prevention in high-risk patients • Suitable for use with custom trays
